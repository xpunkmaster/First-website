Clinical Laboratory Medicine

A complex multidisciplinary field

Clinical Laboratory Medicine or CLM equips health professional with the essential knowledge and skills applicable to different laboratory sectors.

Different Laboratory Sectors

This multidisciplinary field of science consists of different sectors where lab proffesionals perform the routine or specialized tests for diagnosis of various health disorder or diseases. According to the field of specialization various fields include:

  1. Clinical Microbiology
  2. Clinical Biochemistry
  3. Clinical Hematology
  4. Blood- banking
  5. Serology
  6. Histo/Cytopathology
  7. Molecular biology and genetics
